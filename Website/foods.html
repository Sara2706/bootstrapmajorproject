Foods in Ireland!

There is some of the irish food's!


Traditional irish breakfast

A "Full Irish" breakfast consists of sausages, rashers(bacon), eggs, black pudding, white puddingand toast..

Often there will be a grilled tomato as well as mushrooms,and baked beans.

And of course, no breakfast is complete without a lovely potof tea!


Bangers and Mash

This classic of sausages, mashed potato and gravy is not uniqueto Ireland, but what makes it special is theIrishsausages. Most countries have their own way of makingsausages,and they are one thing I miss from home if I'm awaytravelling!
